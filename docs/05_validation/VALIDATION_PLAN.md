# Validation Plan

How do we know the custom DS 100F enclosure works?

## 1. Structural Validation
- **Mass Check:** Total assembly must weigh ≤ 4.5 kg before mounting.
- **Modal Check:** Shell should not exhibit audible rattles or "oil-canning" when stimulated with a 40–200 Hz sweep at 90 dB.
- **Mounting Check:** Bracket must hold angle without drooping under vibration.

## 2. Acoustic Validation (REW)
- **Port Tuning:** Impedance sweep of the final enclosure must show an impedance saddle matching the original DS 100F tuning frequency (extracted during teardown).
- **Frequency Response:** Near-field (1m) measurement must achieve ±3 dB window from ~75 Hz to 18 kHz, matching the donor voicing.
- **Air Leaks:** No audible chuffing from panel seams during 50 Hz sine wave excitation at 20 W.

## 3. Manufacturing Validation
- **Draft Angle Analysis:** All vacuum-formed faces must pass a ≥ 3° draft check in Fusion 360.
- **Placing Volume:** The shell blank must fit entirely within the 430 × 280 mm Formech 300XQ envelope unless a multi-part split is defined.
